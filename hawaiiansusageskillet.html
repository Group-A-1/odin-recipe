Hawaiian Sausage Skillet Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Hawaiian sausage, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) pineapple chunks, drained
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice (optional, for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced Hawaiian sausage and cook until browned.
  3. Add diced bell pepper, onion, and minced garlic. Saut√© until vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in drained pineapple chunks, soy sauce, and brown sugar. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions.
  7. Serve over cooked rice if desired.
